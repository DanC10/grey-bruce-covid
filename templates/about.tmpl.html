About

Grey Bruce COVID-19 tracker is a tool to keep track of the spread of the 2019 novel coronavirus (COVID-19) in the Grey Bruce public health region. The data used in this app was acquired from the government of Ontario's website using their CKAN API. This can be found here: https://data.ontario.ca/dataset/confirmed-positive-cases-of-covid-19-in-ontario

The "Accurate Episode Date" is described by the iPHIS (integrated Public Health Information System) as an estimate of the onset date of disease for a case of COVID-19.

The Accurate Episode Date is an estimate of the onset date of disease for a case. In order to determine this date, the following hierarchy is in place in iPHIS:
  • Onset Date
  • Specimen Collection Date
  • Laboratory Test Date
  • Reported Date
If an Onset Date exists, it will be used in place of the Specimen Collection Date followed by the other episode dates in the hierarchy; however, the earliest date available at each stage of the hierarchy is selected as the Accurate Episode Date. For example, the earlier of two specimen collection dates would be selected as the episode accurate date at the second stage of the hierarchy for a case that has two specimen collection dates, but no symptom onset date in iPHIS. Episode Date Hierarchy prevents cases from being pulled forward in time beyond their Reported Date.

Please note that the government of Ontario updates their data once daily at 10:30AM, and is accurate as of the previous 24 hours.
